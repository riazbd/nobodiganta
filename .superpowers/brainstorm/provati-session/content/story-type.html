What kind of story format are we building?

Choose the format(s) that match your vision for Provati

A

Photo Essay / Gallery Story

A curated collection of photos with captions that together tell a narrative — like a photojournalism piece. Example: "৫০টি ছবিতে ঐতিহাসিক নির্বাচন" (The historic election in 50 photos). Displayed as a full-screen slideshow or scrollable grid with captions.

B

Video Report / Package

A standard video article — one main video with a title, description, and related text. Works like a news article but the primary content is the video itself. Example: YouTube-embed style with transcript or summary below.

C

Mixed Media Story

An article that weaves photos and/or videos inline with text — like a visual longform piece. Photos and videos appear at specific points within the text body, creating a rich multimedia reading experience.

D

All of the above — separate content types

Build Photo Story, Video Story, and Mixed Media as three distinct article types, each with its own admin UI and public display template.
